understand the system
why AllDone exists
Most productivity tools help you capture more. AllDone solves a different problem: how do you actually finish things, and how do you make sure the urgent never completely crowds out the important?
the double tick
Every task has three states. Tap the circle once to mark it started — this signals momentum even when you can't finish yet. Tap again to mark it done — green, confetti, coins awarded. Tap a third time to reset.
No strikethroughs. No red. No failure language anywhere in the app.
your execution score
Every task is worth 1 point regardless of its tag. Score = tasks done ÷ total tasks × 100. A started task contributes 0.5. Routines are tracked separately.
investment coins 🪙
Coins measure how much of your time went toward your future self. Strategic tags earn coins when you start or complete them.
🔥 Urgent0 coins always
💼 Deep Work1 on start · 3 on done
🧠 Mind & Craft2 on start · 5 on done
🌿 Body & Soul2 on start · 5 on done
🏡 Home & Heart2 on start · 5 on done
✨ Passions3 on start · 8 on done
Coins are awarded on Start too — because protecting time for deep work on a chaotic day deserves recognition, even if you don't finish.
two streaks
🔥 Execution streak counts any day where your score is ≥ 50%, or all your Urgent tasks are done. 🪙 Investment streak counts any day where you completed at least one coin-earning task. They are tracked separately because a 12-day execution streak with a 0-day investment streak is a pattern worth seeing.
your data
Everything lives in a Google Sheet in your own Drive. You own it completely. AllDone just reads and writes to it. Open the Sheet directly anytime — every row is human-readable.
about the 🪙 on tag chips
When you see a 🪙 symbol on a tag chip in your task list, it's a reminder that completing this task earns investment coins — not pressure, just visibility.
